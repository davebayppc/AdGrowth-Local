The "Local Dominator" Campaign Structure

Google Ads Search Architecture

The Philosophy: Stop blending brand terms with discovery terms. I isolate exactly what users are searching for based on intent, ensuring zero budget is wasted on broad matches.

The Workflow: Using Google Ads Editor, I deploy single-intent ad groups (SIAGs) matched with hyper-specific localized landing pages. This structure naturally lowers CPCs by maximizing Quality Score.

  • Strict negative keyword sculpting
  • Exact & Phrase match segregation
  • Automated rule bidding based on target CPA

Data Auditing & Cleanup Protocol

Conversion Tracking & CRM Integration

The Philosophy: If you can't track it, it didn't happen. Most local businesses suffer from "dirty data"—tracking spam calls or bot forms as real leads, which poisons the AI bidding algorithm.

The Workflow: Before spending a dime, I implement robust server-side tracking, offline conversion imports, and CallRail integrations. I feed the algorithm only true, qualified offline sales data.

  • Value-based bidding models
  • Offline Conversion Tracking (OCT) setup
  • Spam filtering and dynamic number insertion
